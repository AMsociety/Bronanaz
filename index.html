Premise

Brace yourself for a comedic thrill like no other and get ready to laugh until your sides split, but beware, this is not your regular fruit salad…it’s a hilariously twisted and psychoactive adventure that only the Bronanaz could take you on!

Bronanaz (a half hour adult animated comedy) follows the outrageous exploits, zany adventures, and unbelievable stupidity of two best friends, a couple of bananas, or as we like to call them, the Bronanaz!

Join these nutty fruits as they indulge in smoking, drinking, cursing, and recklessly embark on absurd adventures alongside their mischievous pet monkey—all while inventing a time machine out of a couch. Stoned out of their peels every step of the way, the Bronanaz will journey across the world, plunge into the ocean, conquer outer space, and probe their way into the uncharted territories of time itself!
